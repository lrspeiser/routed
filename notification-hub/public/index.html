Having trouble opening on macOS?

Until the app is signed, macOS may block it. You can allow it by removing the quarantine attribute:

  1. Open the DMG and drag Routed.app to Applications.
  2. Open Terminal and run:
xattr -rd com.apple.quarantine /Applications/Routed.app

Then launch Routed from Applications. If it still warns you, right-click the app and choose Open.
